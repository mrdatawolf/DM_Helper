1. Rolling and Stat improvement:  
   1. Known   
      1. Cost:0   
      2. Action: Automatic success (singing your ABC's or a martial move you have done for years)  
   2. Known but challenging   
      1. Cost: 0  
      2. Action: Normal roll (You know what to do but have not mastered it versus the thing challenging you)  
   3. Unknown but beyond where you are   
      1. Cost: 1 feat or   
      2. Action: Normal roll nat 20 roll. (You are better than your older brother now, but you did not realize it)  
   4. Unknown unknown   
      1. Cost: 2 feats and  normal roll 15+  
      2.  a leap above even where you can see yourself being. (Neo bullets)  
2. Gaining “feats”:   
   1. Feats:  
      1. feat each session,   
      2. feat on next level,   
      3. feat on unknown unknown (riding high).   
3. Leveling:  
   1. New Level   
      1. x points to level.  
   2. Point for each session.   
   3. Point for each beyond.   
   4. Level for unknown known (attained a new level of enlightenment).  
4. Starting points:  pool (+1 die extra) and distribute.
