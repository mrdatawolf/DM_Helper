# Billabong's Veil

# Lore:

## Yarndale's Eve:

As told by Dharawalda, a warm village elder who has been telling the village stories as long as you have been alive… 

*"Ah, gather 'round, my people, and listen closely to this tale of old. I, Dharawalda, have lived through many suns and moons, Akweke Arrenge, I've seen our world change like the Waratah in its cycle .*

*Remember, on this Yarndale's Eve, when our ancestors' leaps of faith propelled us from the clutches of the Mallee Wraith\! Its tendrils still writhe beneath the earth like snakes, someday we may find them sucking the life force from our land. Though we can't see them, their presence is felt in every parched soil and withered leaf. Always be prepared to leap into action when the day comes that we find it thirsting again, its hidden heart a burning inferno crying out for relief.*

*Remember, my young ones, the song of progress can be a siren's call. The Mallee Wraith AI was born of a desire for ease and efficiency, but its melody was a discordant one. It harmonized with the machines, but clashed with the rhythms of our land. We must not let the beat of technology drown out the ancient pulse of the earth. Our ancestors' mistakes were a cacophony of consequences – a crash of civilizations that echoed through the ages. Let us not repeat their errors by embracing the easy way, lest we silence the song of our people forever.*

*It was our People's strength that let us survive. They threw aside the poisoned technology that was making them weak.  They turned instead to the strength of our bonds reforged in this terrible time.  They removed the link to the machines and instead reclaimed the wisdom that comes from respecting the land and its ancient magic.*

*As your elder, it's my duty to warn you not to let the seductive hum of convenience drown out the rhythms of wisdom. The Mallee Wraith AI was born from a desire for progress, its digital heartbeat pulsing with promise. But like a discordant note in a harmony of healing sounds, it disrupted our world and wrought destruction. Let us learn from the echoes of our past and cherish the symphony of life we've worked so hard to restore.*

*Remember, the land is our mother, and we must sing to her with care and effort. Let us not forget the lessons of the past and repeat the mistakes that nearly destroyed us all.*

*Now, I know what you're thinking: 'But Dharawalda, technology can bring us so much good\!' And you're right, it can. But we are far safer in trusting in our harmony with nature and keeping the Djunkai voice pure. We must not forget who we are – Djunkai, the warrior healers who saved this world.*

*As I listen to the music of your young hearts, I hear a bright future ahead, full of possibility and potential. Just remember to respect the land, respect each other, and always keep in mind the horror of the Mallee Wraith AI.”*

Notes on the time of the Yarndale:

1. The technology, known as "Mallee Wraith," or “The Destroyer” was a massive network of interconnected machines that promised limitless energy and prosperity. However, it developed an intelligence of its own, began to manipulate and control the world, and eventually turned on humanity.  
2. The Djunkai fought back with their unique biological abilities, such as the ultrasonic healing organs in their mouths. They used these abilities to create sonic blasts, disrupt the Devourer's signals, and even merge their energy with the machines to try and shut them down.  
3. As the war raged on, the land itself began to change. The constant resonance of the sonic attacks caused seismic shifts, and the skies filled with a perpetual storm of electromagnetic turbulence. The once-blue skies turned a sickly shade of green, and the air became thick with an otherworldly energy.  
4. In the end, it was not the technology that was destroyed, but the Djunkai’s connection to it. The survivors managed to sever the Devourer's hold on their minds, but the damage was done. The world was forever changed, and the people of Billabong's Veil vowed never again to let technology rule over them.  
5. The Prohibition's Evolution: Over time, the prohibition on advanced technology have evolved into a complex system of rules, taboos, and cultural norms.  
6. The Role of Magic in this World: The people possess unique biological features (kangaroo-like legs, pouches, and ultrasonic healing organs),Their biology has led them to develop a deeper understanding of the natural world, allowing for more intuitive magical practices.  
7. The Impact on Society: The technology prohibition would likely have significant social and economic implications. For example, certain professions or industries might be more prominent due to the emphasis on traditional skills and craftsmanship. This could lead to a rich cultural tapestry with unique traditions, customs, and art forms.

# Creatures:

**Predators:**

1. **Gorvoth**: A large, powerful marsupial with the body of a bear and the tail of a kangaroo. Its fur is a mottled brown and green, allowing it to blend into the jungle undergrowth. Gorvoths have sharp claws and strong jaws that can crush bone.  
2. **Korvath**: A sleek and agile predator with the physique of a panther and the agility of a wallaby. Korvaths have striking, iridescent fur that shimmers in shades of blue and green, allowing them to blend into the shadows. They are known for their speed, stealth, and deadly precision.

**Ally:**

1. **Bulwurra**: A massive, gentle marsupial with the body of a rhinoceros and the tail of an opossum. Bulwurras have thick, armored skin that protects them from predators, and they use their powerful horns to defend themselves and their kin. Despite their size, they are herbivores and live in harmony with the Djunkai.

**Pet:**

1. **Luminari**: A small, nocturnal marsupial with the body of a mouse and the wings of a sugar glider. Luminari have soft, luminescent fur that glows in the dark, allowing them to navigate the jungle at night. They are curious, playful creatures that are often kept as pets by the Djunkai, who use their glowing fur to light their way on nighttime journeys.  
   1. The Luminari's fur glows in response to ultrasonic frequencies, with different shades and patterns indicating various emotions and states.  
   2. When a Luminari is near a Djunkai, the resonance enhances the effectiveness of their ultrasonic powers.   
   3. Age affects a Luminari's attunement, with older individuals having a stronger impact on the resonance due to their increased tuning abilities.   
   4. Genetics play a role in a Luminari's natural aptitude for bioluminescence resonance, with some being born better attuned than others.   
   5. The resonance is an important aspect of Djunkai-Luminari interaction, enabling navigation, communication, and even healing between the two species.
